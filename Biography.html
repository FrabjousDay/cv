Some biographical details

  1. Current editor of The Greyton Mail, for which newspaper I write a nutrition/health column.
  2. Founder member and current Secretary of the Greyton Farm Animal Sanctuary (a registered NPO in South Africa).
  3. Co-builder of 6 strawbale and clay brick houses and barns, 5 of which are on Tabularasa Farm, the home of the farm animal sanctuary.
  4. Founder member and former Secretary of Greyton Transition Town, the first such sustainability organization in Africa.
  5. Completed the Design Course and Practical Internship in Permaculture.
  6. Keen vegetable and herb gardener; I use our garden-grown basil, cilantro, parsley, arugula, chilis and garlic to make preservative-free sauces and pestos.
  7. Producer of fermented foods like green and purple sauerkraut.
  8. Baker of sourdough breads and pizzas in a home-built, wood-fired clay oven.
  9. A cattle rancher and professional hunter in my youth, I've been vegan since the beginning of 2009... better late than never...
  10. Active trail runner, hiker and mountain biker.
  11. A latecomer to the field, I'm trying to become a full-stack web developer... this website being my first design project.
  12. Keen but excruciatingly bad singer, bass guitarist and harmonica player... my greatest regrets, except...
  13. Landlocked instructor-level scuba diver who misses the sea, the most beautiful part of our beautiful blue world.
